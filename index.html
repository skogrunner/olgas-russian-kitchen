Olga's Russian Kitchen

Welcome to Olga's Russian Kitchen!

Olga was born in Kazakhstan and lived in various parts of the Soviet Union during her childhood, as the daughter of a military man. She has worked as a mechanic, engineer, policewoman, and data specialist. Since immigrating to the United States, she has discovered a new passion - to share her love of Russian cuisine with you! Olga is constantly inventing new recipes and tweeking old ones to bring the best of Russian cooking to your table.
